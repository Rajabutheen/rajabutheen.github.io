ACT Fibernet

Elevating the Utility App Experience.

  •   Duration

    3 months

  •   Domain

    Telecommunication

  •   Role

    Art Direction, UI Design

OVERVIEW

ACT Fibernet, one of India’s top broadband providers, set out to revamp its mobile app to better serve 2M+ users across 30+ cities, as the existing version no longer met their evolving goals.

I led the UI design, managing a small team while staying hands-on in crafting key screens. From defining the visual direction to ensuring accessibility and brand alignment, the redesign elevated the user experience and earned international recognition from the A' Design and DNA Paris Awards.

THE GROUNDWORK

Brand-First Design

I started by understanding ACT’s brand personality to ensure the new product design would align with its core identity. This foundation helped maintain brand consistency while creating experiences that resonate with their target audience.

Understanding the Audience

To shape an experience that truly resonates, I focused on identifying the right user groups. This included analyzing audience demographics, usage patterns, and their go-to apps in daily routines providing a foundation to design with clarity, relevance, and purpose.

Competitor Aesthetic Analysis

To uncover opportunities for differentiation, I examined the visual language of key competitors. This analysis provided insights into industry patterns and helped identify gaps we could leverage ensuring ACT’s new design not only stood out visually but also set a new benchmark in user experience.

DEFINING THE VISUAL DIRECTION

Grounded in ACT’s brand values, mission,
and audience insights, I shaped

a visual direction that aligned with the brand’s personality and purpose. The goal was to craft a cohesive and memorable identity building recognition, trust, and deeper engagement through every interaction.

Choosing Typefaces

To reflect the brand’s personality and visual identity, I selected Jost as the primary display typeface. Its clean, modern, and vibrant character aligned seamlessly with the chosen visual direction. Paired with Hanken Grotesk a geometric typeface with a large x-height the combination enhances legibility and readability across digital screens.

Custom Icons & Illustrations

Directed the creation of bespoke icons and illustrations to bring personality to key features making the UI feel fresh, vibrant, and true to the brand’s visual language.

Color System Expansion

Expanded the palette using double split-complementary harmony to meet functional needs, ensuring all UI colors comply with WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards.

UI DESIGN

To make everyday tasks feel effortless, I reimagined the UI with clarity and purpose. Every element from hierarchy to interaction was crafted to guide users intuitively, helping them navigate with ease and confidence.

WHAT USERS SAY

Understanding user sentiment is key to any product’s success. Positive feedback is not just validation it energizes the entire team. The revamped app received encouraging reviews on the Play Store, reinforcing the impact of our design decisions.
